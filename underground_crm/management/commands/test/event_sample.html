Conspiracy night (Melbourne) Conspiracy night (Melbourne) Conspiracy night (Melbourne) Conspiracy night (Melbourne)

Posted by Owen Miller on 26 April 2026

A cabal is meeting on Saturday 16 May to reminisce about past conspiracies that have worked, and to float some alternative histories about conspiracies that could have been. Only the most discerning political operatives will be able to tell these truths from fiction.

Fusion needs to send operatives to join in the evening's socialising and maybe present one of these conspiracies themselves. The members of the cabal will be able to reveal themselves to each other by discerning a consistent truth.

Will we be able to uncover who's really running the world?

The presentations will take course during an evening of socialising over drinks and hors d'œuvres − feel free to bring a friend to the rendez-vous.

When

May 16, 2026 at 18:00 - 11pm (NSW/ACT/VIC/TAS timezone)

Where

Hanging Gardens of Brunswick, 701/5 Ovens Street, Brunswick 3056
