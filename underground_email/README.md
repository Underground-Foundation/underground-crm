# Underground Email
At https://www.smtp2go.com/comparison/, there is a useful comparison of SMTP2Go versus other popular providers.

My understanding is that SendGrid is the market leader, so I was inherently reluctant to depend on them. Going
with them would contribute to their potential monopoly power and I'm fearful of being locked in, especially if
they were to raise their prices.

Another thing to consider is that SMPT2Go is [headquartered in New Zealand](https://www.smtp2go.com/about/). Yes,
they're still part of the [Five Eyes](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Five_Eyes), but I'm still more suspicious of
the United States, so I'm hesitant to choose SendGrid (owned by 
American [Twilio](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Twilio)).

SMTP2Go offers [1,000 free emails per month](https://www.smtp2go.com/pricing/), which is a useful allowance for the 
Fusion Party, since we send emails every ~2 months. SendGrid offers a 
[60-day trial](https://www.twilio.com/en-us/products/email-api/pricing) of 100 emails / day.

Read through their API documentation at https://developers.smtp2go.com/docs/introduction-guide.
